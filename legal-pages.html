Terms for Buyers

By using DropCart Africa, buyers agree to the following terms and conditions:

  • Accurate Information: Provide accurate details (name, phone, address, landmark) for successful order delivery.
  • Payment Terms: Pay for products upon delivery unless otherwise stated in specific product listings.
  • Inspection: Inspect goods at the point of delivery and report any issues within 24 hours.
  • Price Changes: Understand that product prices and availability may change without prior notice.
  • Referral Codes: Use referral codes responsibly. Any fraudulent use may lead to account suspension.
  • Order Cancellation: Orders can be cancelled within 12 hours of placement. After this period, cancellation may incur a fee.
  • Return Policy: Products can be returned within 7 days if they are defective or significantly different from their description.
  • Communication: Maintain a valid phone number for delivery coordination and updates.
  • Account Security: Maintain the security of your account credentials and notify us immediately of any unauthorized use.

These terms are subject to change. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.

Terms for Vendors

Vendors on DropCart Africa agree to the following terms and conditions:

  • Accurate Information: Submit accurate business and product information during registration.
  • Product Standards: Ensure all products comply with Nigerian and international standards.
  • Admin Approval: Accept that all products must be admin-approved before going live on the platform.
  • Logistics: Deliver products promptly to DropCart Africa's logistics partners when required.
  • Prohibited Items: Avoid listing counterfeit, illegal, or harmful products. Violations will lead to termination.
  • Commission Structure: Agree to the platform's commission structure for each sale.
  • Product Accuracy: Ensure product descriptions, images, and specifications accurately represent the actual items.
  • Inventory Management: Maintain accurate inventory levels and promptly update product availability.
  • Customer Service: Respond to customer inquiries and complaints in a timely and professional manner.
  • Account Termination: Understand that DropCart Africa reserves the right to terminate vendor accounts for violations of these terms.

These terms are subject to change. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.

Terms for Resellers (Referral Agents)

Resellers on DropCart Africa agree to the following terms and conditions:

  • Referral Codes: Share only their unique referral code for promotions.
  • Commission Eligibility: Earn commissions solely on valid purchases made through their referral link/code.
  • Ethical Practices: Not manipulate orders or engage in self-purchases to inflate commissions.
  • Commission Tracking: Accept that commissions are calculated automatically and visible in their dashboard.
  • Payment Methods: Withdraw earnings only through official company-approved payment methods.
  • Representation: Not misrepresent themselves as employees or official representatives of DropCart Africa.
  • Marketing Materials: Use only approved marketing materials and make no false claims about products or services.
  • Minimum Withdrawal: Understand that commission withdrawals are subject to minimum amount requirements.
  • Commission Rates: Accept that commission rates may vary by product category and are subject to change.
  • Account Termination: Understand that DropCart Africa reserves the right to terminate reseller accounts for violations of these terms.

These terms are subject to change. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.

Privacy Policy

DropCart Africa values your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Information Collection

We collect buyer, vendor, and reseller information only for order fulfillment, communication, and account management. This includes:

  • Contact information (name, email, phone number)
  • Delivery addresses and landmarks
  • Payment information
  • Order history and preferences
  • Business information for vendors

Information Usage

We use your information to:

  • Process and fulfill orders
  • Communicate about order status and updates
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Manage vendor and reseller accounts
  • Track referrals and commissions

Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We only share information with:

  • Trusted logistics partners for delivery purposes
  • Payment processors for transaction handling
  • Legal authorities when required by law

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

Cookies

We use cookies to improve site performance and user experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications

Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries or to exercise your rights, please contact our support team at support@dropcartafrica.com.

This privacy policy is subject to change. We will notify users of significant changes through our website or email.
